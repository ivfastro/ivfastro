RUDRAKSHA BEADS


 The Rudraksha bead may have one or several faces and each one confers specific benefits. Ancient text reveals the beads of  Rudraksha have immense amazing powers. These beads are said to be a gift from the Almighty to his children.
 

Ek Mukhi (1-face) Rudraksha
Price: $52.00 USD
Ruling Planet-Sun
One face rudraksha is a royal rudraksha representing the supreme reality. The individual and the family who possesses the bead is really fortunate. It is like celestial cow, kamdhenu, that helps to fulfils all the desires of the wearer.

Two Mukhi (2-face) Rudraksha
Price: $8.00 USD
Ruling Planet: Moon
This bead represents Ardhnareshwar, Lord Rudra and his consort Goddess Parvati. It fosters unity and harmony between those who are divided. Couple who wear this remains united.
